Micros Apps Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 07, 2024

Micros Apps ("Application") is the official application from the makers of Micros devices ("We" or "Us"). This application serves as a companion to Micros performance measurement devices with the purpose of helping users access, manage, and analyze vehicle performance data on authorized racetracks and closed courses.

We highly value user privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. By using this application, you agree to the following privacy policy:

⚠️ Important Safety Notice

This app and Micros device are designed exclusively for use on authorized racetracks, closed courses, and private property where permitted. Users must comply with all applicable traffic laws and regulations. We do not condone or support illegal street racing or any unsafe driving activities on public roads.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data (GPS/Wi-Fi–based)

The app requests location permission ("When In Use") for the following specific purposes:

  • WiFi Device Detection: To detect and verify connection to your Micros performance measurement device via WiFi network (network names starting with "micros")
  • Custom Track Configuration: To enable you to configure authorized racetrack layouts by marking start/finish points on a map for track day events
  • Session Start Point: To set the performance measurement session start point location on the map for track configuration on authorized venues

Important: Your location data is:

  • Only used locally on your device
  • Only accessed when you are actively using the app
  • NOT transmitted to our servers or any external services
  • NOT shared with any third parties
  • NOT used for tracking, advertising, or analytics
  • NOT tracked in the background when the app is closed

Local Network Access

The app requires local network access to communicate with your Micros performance measurement device:

  • Device Communication: Connect to Micros device at IP address 192.168.52.1 over local WiFi
  • Data Synchronization: Retrieve performance measurement data, lap times, and speed metrics from the device
  • Device Management: Send track configurations and settings to the device

This communication occurs only within your local WiFi network and does not leave your device or network.

Device Data

Technical data exchanged between the app and Micros devices, such as:

  • Device name, serial number, model, and firmware version
  • Battery status and GPS signal strength
  • Performance metrics (lap times, speed, distance, elevation)
  • Track configurations and settings

This data is used solely for device operation and displaying performance statistics.

Locally Stored App Data

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Performance measurement session history (acceleration tests and lap times)
  • Custom authorized track configurations
  • App settings and preferences
  • Device connection information

We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, or financial information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Detecting and connecting to your Micros device via WiFi
  • Creating and managing custom authorized track configurations for track day events
  • Setting performance measurement session start points on the map
  • Displaying and managing performance measurement results locally on your device
  • Ensuring proper device functionality and synchronization
  • Improving your user experience with the app

The data is never used for advertising, analytics, marketing, profiling, or any other purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data stays exclusively on your device using secure database storage
  • No External Transmission: No data is sent to external servers or cloud services
  • No Cloud Backup: We do not use cloud storage or backup services
  • Secure Communication: Device communication uses local WiFi connection only (192.168.52.1)
  • Data Protection: We use standard iOS security features to protect your data
  • Automatic Deletion: All app data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

4. No Tracking and No Third-Party Sharing

No Tracking

The application does not track users across apps, websites, or services.

We do not:

  • Use advertising identifiers or tracking cookies
  • Link user data with third-party data for tracking
  • Use data for targeted ads, marketing, or behavioral profiling
  • Use third-party analytics SDKs or tracking tools
  • Monitor user behavior outside the app
  • Collect device identifiers for tracking purposes

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or upload any of your data to third parties.

Data communication occurs only with:

  • Your Micros device: Direct local WiFi connection for device operation
  • Map Services: When creating custom track configurations, map providers (Google Maps/Apple Maps) may access location according to their own privacy policies

Data may be disclosed only if required by law with proper legal authority.

5. Third-Party Services

Map Services

The app uses map services for the custom track configuration feature:

  • Map providers may collect location data according to their own privacy policies
  • This occurs only when you actively use the custom track configuration feature
  • Please refer to Google Maps or Apple Maps privacy policy for their data practices

No Other Third-Party Services

We do NOT use:

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks or SDKs
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media integrations
  • Cloud storage services

6. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Location Permission: You can revoke location access at any time through iOS Settings → Micros App → Location
  • Local Network: You can disable local network access through iOS Settings (some features may be unavailable)
  • Delete Session History: Delete local performance measurement session data at any time within the app
  • Uninstall App: All data is automatically and permanently deleted when you uninstall the app
  • No Account Required: The app does not require account creation or personal identification
  • Stop Using Anytime: You can stop using the app at any time without any data retention

7. iOS Specific Information

Permissions Required:

  • Location (When In Use): Required to detect Micros WiFi device and configure custom tracks. Location tracking does NOT continue in the background when the app is closed.
  • Local Network: Required to communicate with Micros device over WiFi at 192.168.52.1

When Location is Accessed:

  • Only when you are actively using the app
  • When you tap "Connect" to pair with Micros device
  • When you create or edit custom track configurations on the map
  • Never in the background or when the app is closed

8. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for use by motorsport enthusiasts for vehicle performance measurement on authorized racetracks and closed courses, and requires a physical Micros performance measurement device to function.

9. Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as you have the app installed. When you uninstall the app, all data is automatically and permanently deleted from your device. We do not retain any copies of your data on our servers.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, we will notify users through the app or our official channels with an updated "Last Updated" date. The app will continue to operate without server-based data collection unless users are explicitly informed and consent to any changes.
