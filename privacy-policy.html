Micros Apps Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09/19/2025

Micros Apps ("Application") is the official application from the makers of Micros devices ("We" or "Us"). This application serves as a companion to Micros devices with the purpose of helping users access, manage, and analyze race data.

We highly value user privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. By using this application, you agree to the following privacy policy:

1. Information We Collect

Location Data (GPS/Wi-Fiâ€“based)

The app accesses approximate or precise location only to:

  • set the race start point on the map
  • connect and communicate with Micros devices via Wi-Fi

Location data is not stored, not uploaded, and not shared with any third party.

Device Data

Technical data exchanged between the app and Micros devices, such as:

  • device settings
  • status information
  • race metrics

This data is used solely for device operation.

Locally Stored App Data

The app may store race history or session information only on your device.

We do not collect personal data such as names, emails, addresses, phone numbers, or financial information.

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • connecting the app with Micros hardware
  • setting race start points
  • displaying and managing race results locally
  • ensuring proper device functionality

The data is never used for advertising, analytics, marketing, or profiling.

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data stays only on your device.
  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No cloud storage is used.
  • We use standard device security features to protect your data.

4. No Tracking and No Third-Party Sharing

No Tracking

The application does not track users across apps, websites, or services.

We do not:

  • use advertising identifiers
  • link user data with third-party data
  • use data for ads, marketing, or behavioral profiling
  • use third-party analytics SDKs

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or upload any data to third parties.

Data is shared only with:

  • Micros device hardware (direct device connection), or
  • if required by law (rare and only with legal authority)

5. User Control and Rights

Users may:

  • delete local race history at any time
  • stop using the app at any time
  • disable location access (but some features may be unavailable)

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will notify users through the app or our official channels. The app will continue to operate without server-based data collection unless users are explicitly informed otherwise.
